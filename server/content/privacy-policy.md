# Privacy Policy

Last updated April 19, 2026

This app is used to create and share short links. This privacy policy explains what information this deployment collects, how it is used, and the choices available to users.

## Information We Collect

- Account information such as your name, email address, and profile picture if you sign in with Google.
- Email address information if you sign in using an email code.
- Content and settings you create in the app, including links, destinations, tags, variables, and scheduling settings.
- Basic usage and security information such as sign-in events, link opens, timestamps, and similar operational logs.
- Cookies or similar session data needed to keep you signed in and operate the service securely.

## How We Use Information

- To let you sign in and use the app.
- To create, manage, and redirect short links.
- To provide analytics, prevent abuse, and keep the service secure.
- To support account administration for invited users.

## How Information Is Shared

Information is shared only as needed to operate this deployment, such as with hosting, authentication, email, and infrastructure providers. Information may also be visible to the administrators of this deployment. This app is not intended to sell personal information.

## Third-Party Destinations

Short links created in this app may send you to third-party websites. Those websites have their own privacy practices, and this policy does not control how those third parties use your data.

## Data Retention

Account information, links, settings, and usage records may be kept for as long as needed to operate the service, maintain security, comply with legal obligations, or support the people using this deployment.

## Your Choices

If you want your account data, links, or settings changed or removed, contact the person who invited you or the administrator of this deployment.

## Contact

For privacy questions about this deployment, contact the app operator or administrator who provided you access.
